Project description

This project is focusing on building a solution and model to help classify text messages related to and following real-world disaster scenarios. Typically after a larger disaster thousands or even millions of messages are being send out. This is also the time when the disaster response organisations have the least capacity to filter and handle the messages that are the most important as well as relevant for those specific organisations.

The data that we are working with are within the genres: direct, news or social media messages and comes from several different disasters and has been labeled and captured by Figure Eight.
